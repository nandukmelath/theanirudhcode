Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 May 2026

Your privacy matters. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it. By using theanirudhcode, you agree to these terms.

What we collect

  • Account information: Name, email, phone, password (hashed).
  • Demographic data: Date of birth, sex, country, city.
  • Health information: Goals, conditions, medications, lifestyle (collected during intake, optional unless booking).
  • Preferences: Communication channel, language, marketing opt-in.
  • Usage data: Login times, appointments booked, pages visited.

Why we collect it

  • Consultation delivery: Personalised protocols, dosing, safety screening.
  • Communication: Appointment reminders, follow-ups, prescription delivery.
  • Service improvement: Aggregate analytics, content relevance, program design.
  • Marketing (opt-in only): Newsletter, new program announcements.

How we protect it

  • All data encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest (Google Cloud SQL).
  • Passwords hashed with bcrypt — never stored in plaintext.
  • Access restricted to Dr. Anirudh and authorised staff under confidentiality agreement.
  • Health records are never sold, shared with advertisers, or used outside care delivery.

Your rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your data anytime by emailing dranirudh@theanirudhcode.com.
  • Correction: Update inaccuracies via your account dashboard.
  • Deletion: Request full account deletion. Data removed within 30 days (some records retained per medical record laws).
  • Marketing opt-out: Unsubscribe link in every email.

Third-party services

We use the following processors strictly to deliver service:

  • Google Cloud — hosting, database, email (Gmail API).
  • Razorpay / Stripe — payment processing (PCI-DSS compliant).
  • Google Calendar — appointment scheduling.
  • WhatsApp Business API — reminders (opt-in only).

Compliance

We aim to comply with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023, GDPR (for EU residents), and HIPAA-style protections for health data. For specific concerns or rights requests, contact dranirudh@theanirudhcode.com.

Updates

We may revise this policy. Material changes will be notified via email and posted here with a new "Last updated" date.
