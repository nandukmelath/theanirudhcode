The Protocols

The Morning Reset
Protocol

Inspired by Ayurvedic Dinacharya — the science of daily routine — this 45-minute morning ritual primes your nervous system, activates digestion, and sets your circadian rhythm for the entire day.

1
5:30–6am: Wake before sunrise. Oil pulling + tongue scraping to detox overnight buildup.
2
Hydration: 500ml warm water with lemon + a pinch of rock salt — activates digestive fire (Agni).
3
Breathwork: 10 minutes of Nadi Shodhana or Bhramari to regulate the nervous system.
4
Sunlight: 10–15 min direct morning light exposure to set cortisol rhythm and sleep-wake cycles.

The 4R Gut Repair
Framework

The gut is the foundation. Before addressing any other system — hormones, brain, energy — we repair the gut lining, restore healthy bacteria, and remove inflammatory triggers.

R1
Remove: Eliminate gluten, seed oils, processed sugar, and gut-disrupting medications.
R2
Replace: Rebuild digestive enzymes and stomach acid with bitters, apple cider vinegar, and zinc.
R3
Reinoculate: Restore microbiome diversity with fermented foods, prebiotics, and targeted probiotics.
R4
Repair: Heal the gut lining with L-glutamine, collagen broth, slippery elm, and aloe vera.

The Deep Sleep
Architecture Protocol

Sleep is the most powerful anti-aging intervention available. But modern life systematically destroys sleep quality. This protocol rebuilds it from the ground up.

1
Light diet: No screens 90 minutes before bed. Dim all lights after 8pm to preserve melatonin.
2
Temperature: Sleep in a cool room (18–19°C). Cold exposure drops core temperature, deepening sleep stages.
3
Wind-down ritual: Ashwagandha + magnesium glycinate + 4-7-8 breathing to shift into parasympathetic.
4
Consistent timing: Same wake time every day — anchors the circadian rhythm more than bedtime.

The Breathwork
Nervous System Reset

Your breath is the only autonomic function you can consciously control. This makes it a direct dial into your nervous system — capable of shifting you from fight-or-flight to deep healing.

1
Box Breathing: 4-4-4-4 cadence. Used by Navy SEALs. Instantly calms the amygdala and restores focus.
2
Nadi Shodhana: Alternate nostril breathing. Balances left and right hemispheres; reduces cortisol in 5 minutes.
3
Wim Hof Method: 30 power breaths + retention. Activates immune response, increases energy and resilience.
4
4-7-8 Breathing: The most powerful sleep-induction technique. Activates vagal tone within 4 cycles.
