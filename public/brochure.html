About

Why theanirudhcode

Dr. Anirudh is a metabolic and functional health specialist whose practice sits at the intersection of modern biochemistry and ancient Ayurvedic intelligence. He has spent years studying the root causes of chronic conditions — insulin resistance, hormonal imbalance, gut dysfunction, and inflammatory disease — and built a clinical system that addresses them at their source, not just at the surface.

The practice rejects the band-aid model of healthcare. Where conventional medicine often masks symptoms, Dr. Anirudh's approach is built on understanding why the body is signalling distress — and creating the precise conditions for it to reverse course.

Every client receives a bespoke protocol. No templates, no generic advice — a system calibrated to your biology, your lifestyle, and your metabolic data.

"Your body is not broken. It is responding rationally to irrational inputs. Change the inputs, and the body responds."
— Dr. Anirudh
